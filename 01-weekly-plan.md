

## weekly-plan.md

### Weekly Time Budget
- Mon–Fri: 2 hours/day
- Saturday: 2–3 hours (morning only)
- Sunday: Optional (1–2 hours)

### Weekly Structure

**Monday – Concepts**  
Theory only: Kubernetes, system design, cloud concepts

**Tuesday – Hands-on**  
Primary tech of the month (Docker/K8s/Redis/Kafka)

**Wednesday – Debugging**  
Fix issues, refine configs, break & repair

**Thursday – Architecture**  
System design, trade-offs, diagrams

**Friday – Light + AI**  
Blogs, refactoring with AI, review

**Saturday – Deep Work (AM only)**  
Projects, deployments, infra

**Sunday – Optional Review**  
Revision or rest
